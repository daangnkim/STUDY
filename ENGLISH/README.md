## [Pattern]

### 순서대로 A, B, C, D 다.

- In order, they are A, B, C, D
- they are A, B, C, D in sequence

### A, B 중에 무엇을 선택할지 모르겠다.

- I don't know which to choose between A and B.
- I don't know which one to choose, A and B.
- I stuck between A and B.

- Let's assume that ~
